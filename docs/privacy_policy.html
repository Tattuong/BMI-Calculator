Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

BMI Calculator ("the App") helps you calculate and track your body mass index. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

The App stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • BMI calculation history (weight, height, result, timestamp)
  • Unit preference (metric or imperial)
  • Theme preference (light/dark)
  • Language preference
  • Onboarding completion status

We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on external servers.

Fonts

The App may download font files from Google Fonts on first use. This involves a network request to Google; no personal data is shared.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties.

Data Retention & Deletion

All data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear the App's storage.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the developer through the app store listing.
